# ⚡ VILGAX Quick Start - 30 Seconds to Voice Control

## 🚀 Get Started Now

### Step 1: Load the Page
Visit any of these pages:
- `patient.html` - Patient dashboard
- `doctor.html` - Doctor portal
- `admin.html` - Admin panel

### Step 2: Locate VILGAX
Look for the **🤖 purple glowing button** in the **bottom-right corner**

### Step 3: Click to Activate
Click the purple avatar to open the VILGAX panel

### Step 4: Start Speaking
Click **"🎤 Start Listening"** and say your command:
- "Book appointment"
- "Find medicine"
- "Show records"
- "Video call"

Done! VILGAX will execute your command instantly.

---

## 🎤 Voice Commands (Quick Ref)

```
📅 Book appointment      → Opens appointment form
💊 Find medicine        → Shows medicine finder
📋 Show records         → Displays medical records
📞 Video call           → Starts video consultation
💻 My prescriptions     → Opens prescriptions page
❓ Help                 → Shows all commands
❌ Close                → Hides panel
```

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|

## 🎓 Pro Tips

1. **Speak Naturally** - VILGAX understands casual speech
2. **One Command at a Time** - Say one action, wait for response
3. **Try the Buttons** - If voice doesn't work, use quick buttons
4. **Stay Visible** - Keep the VILGAX panel open for best results
5. **Use Context** - VILGAX learns from your commands over time
