## ❓ Common Questions

### "Why does VILGAX block my command?"
This feature is protected (for your privacy). You need to log in first.

### "Can VILGAX understand my accent?"
Yes! VILGAX understands:
- Regional accents
- Formal & informal speech
- Code-switched language (Hinglish/Tanglish)
- Different speeds

### "Is VILGAX available everywhere?"
✅ On patient pages:
- Homepage (index.html)
- Patient Dashboard
- Medicine Finder
- Appointments
- Video Calls
- Prescriptions
- Chatbot

❌ NOT on:
- Doctor pages
- Admin pages
- Login page

### "Can doctors and admins use VILGAX?"
No - it's for **patients only**. Doctors and admins have their own interfaces.

### "My language isn't working"
Try multiple ways to say the same thing:
- Pure: "चिकित्सा रिकॉर्ड"
- Hinglish: "mera record dekho"
- English: "medical records"
